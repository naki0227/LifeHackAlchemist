## ğŸ“– Overview

**Lifehack Alchemist** is a **"Stealth Education" RPG** where an underdog protagonist, who can only use "Household Magic", dominates an alternate world by utilizing modern scientific knowledge (Lifehacks).

Players immerse themselves in the fun of laws of physics and chemical reactions through gameplay without feeling like "studying".
Google Gemini API analyzes the player's free combination of "Materials" x "Magic" x "Conditions" and simulates scientifically correct phenomena in real-time.

### ğŸ—º Story
The stage is a medieval fantasy world where elemental magic is supreme. Reincarnated from modern Japan, the protagonist uses only basic "Household Magic" (Ignition, Cleaning, Drying, Watering).
But he knew. You can enrich uranium with "Cleaning" and cause a dust explosion with "Ignition"...
This is a story of an underdog overturning common sense with scientific knowledge.

---

## ğŸ® Core Loop

1.  **Scavenge:** Pick up "trash" or "common materials" in dungeons.
2.  **Synthesize:** Combine materials with appropriate "living magic" and "conditions" (sealing, compression, etc.).
3.  **AI Judge:** Gemini 2.5 analyzes the combination and simulates scientifically correct phenomena (explosion, freezing, byproducts, etc.).
4.  **Discovery:** Discover new phenomena and unravel the world (and its laws of physics).
